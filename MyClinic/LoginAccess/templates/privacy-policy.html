Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2025

EzyDoc ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, age, gender, date of birth, email address, phone number.
  • Health Information: Uploaded prescriptions, lab reports.
  • Appointment Data: Doctor/lab selection, appointment times.
  • Usage Data: Analytics for app usage and improvements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Manage user registration and appointments.
  • Allow healthcare professionals to access relevant health records.
  • Improve and personalize your app experience.
  • Provide analytics for administrative use (non-identifiable).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Doctors and labs directly involved in your care.
  • Government authorities when legally required.

4. Data Security

We use secured infrastructure hosted on a VPS well protected by firewalls, SSL encryption, and access-controlled MySQL databases to protect your data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data or account.
  • Contact us with any concerns regarding your privacy.
